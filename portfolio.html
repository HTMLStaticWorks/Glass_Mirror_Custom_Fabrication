Manufacturing Tolerances

Understanding our precision capabilities and standard architectural deductions.

Frameless Enclosures

Our CNC machinery cuts to a standard tolerance of ± 1/16 inch. For all out-of-plumb walls, provide width measurements at the bottom, middle, and top of the opening to ensure a custom trapezoidal cut.

Decorative Mirrors

For inset mirrors, deduct exactly 1/8 inch from the tightest opening measurement on both width and height to accommodate standard installation adhesives and thermal expansion.

Precision Guidelines

Shower Enclosure Protocol

Strict measurement standards to ensure perfect frameless installations. Do not assume any architectural surface is perfectly straight.

01

Plumb & Level Verification

Always verify if the walls are plumb and the sill is level using a 4-foot or 6-foot digital level before submitting any dimensions.

02

Sill Sweep Deductions

Standard fabrication deductions include a 3/8" to 1/2" deduction at the bottom for the sweep, depending on the requested hinge system.

03

Centerline Dimensions

For 90-degree and neo-angle showers, always provide measurements strictly along the centerline of the curb where the glass will ultimately rest.

04

Wall-to-Glass Hinging

When measuring for a wall-mounted hinge door, provide the exact tight width. Our engineers will apply the necessary hardware deductions automatically.
